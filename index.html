About me

Turning your ideas into digital realities

I'm Karen, a UX/UI Designer passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences. I bridge the gap between user needs and business goals through thoughtful, research-backed design.

My process is rooted in empathy. I believe the best designs come from truly understanding the people who will use them. Whether it's a complex product or a simple interaction, I obsess over the details that make the difference.

I am also the creator of MossyUI, a more fun way to customize your systems. For the fandoms.
